Payments · Regulation · Partnerships

PayQR — Navigating Regulation to Empower Merchants

The Central Bank mandated a universal QR standard for mobile money — but only banks could issue the codes. As a fintech, we were locked out by regulation while our merchants bled. I turned the constraint into a strategy: partner with a licensed bank, own the merchant experience, and use their rails to issue compliant QR codes under our brand. The result was PayQR — a dynamic, high-contrast QR generator with a Merchant Soundbox for instant audio confirmation, plus a static fallback for low-connectivity markets. Within 30 days of launch, DAU climbed 12% and the churn bleeding stopped.

+12% DAU in 30 days
–15% Merchant churn reversed
All MOMO Providers supported
